Dice of Doom

Click on a tile to make a move.

You must make a move on the first turn. If you can't, the game is over, and the player with the most dice wins.

You receive extra dice at the end of a turn if you capture enough opponent dice.

You are green.
The computer is red.

this is Land of Lisp’s Dice of Doom implemented in coffeescript / node.js.
